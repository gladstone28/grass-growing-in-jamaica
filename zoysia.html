Zoysia Grass

About Zoysia Grass

Zoysia grass is a popular choice for lawns in warmer climates due to its ability to withstand high temperatures and relatively low maintenance requirements. Originating from Southeast Asia, this perennial grass is known for its dense, carpet-like turf and fine texture, which make it an attractive option for both residential and commercial properties.

Zoysia's deep root system allows it to be highly drought-resistant, reducing the need for frequent watering. Additionally, it has a remarkable ability to recover from damage and resists weeds effectively, ensuring a uniform and beautiful lawn throughout the year. Its tolerance to shade, salt, and foot traffic makes it versatile for various landscaping applications, from golf courses to coastal areas.

For optimal growth, Zoysia prefers full sun but can tolerate partial shade. It thrives in a wide range of soil types, provided they are well-drained. Regular mowing to keep it at its recommended height, along with annual dethatching and aeration, will help maintain its health and appearance. While Zoysia is slower to establish than some grasses, its longevity and resilience make it a worthy investment for those seeking a lush, green lawn with minimal upkeep.
